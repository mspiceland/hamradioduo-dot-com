What is Pebble HF?

Pebble HF is an ultra-affordable, ultra-portable HF radio kit designed to get more people into HF amateur radio. It's a single band (20 meters), all-mode capable QRP radio that weighs less than 4.7oz and comes as an easy-to-build kit with almost all parts pre-soldered — most people build it in under an hour.

Key highlights:

  • Ultra-affordable, easy to build kit — only 14 through-hole parts to solder
  • ~5W output with 12V input, ~1W output powered by USB-C 5V
  • CW, SSB (USB/LSB), and digital mode capable
  • Built-in CW key and microphone
  • DSP filters from full open down to 50 Hz
  • Weighs less than 4.7oz (132g)
  • Open source hardware and firmware

Pebble HF was born from a passion to make sure a great, ultra-affordable option exists for anyone wanting to get into HF radio. Whether you're upgrading your license, trying out portable radio, or just want a fun project — Pebble HF is for you.
