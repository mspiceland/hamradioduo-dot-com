Huntsville Hamfest: Why We Love It!

Have you ever wondered why Huntsville Hamfest is such a big deal? It's legendary for being the "Friendliest Hamfest in America," and we're here to show you why!

The Magic of Huntsville

Huntsville Hamfest isn't just about gadgets and gear. It's a place where radio enthusiasts gather to share their passion. This event is different, because in addition to the event, there is a lot of fun to have at the local'lish campground – a youtuber lunch (which benefits the ARRL Teachers Institute), a parachute mobile contact opportunity, stations setup for local 2m CW contacts, and a bunch of hands on "classes" from the Four Days In August, organized by TO.
