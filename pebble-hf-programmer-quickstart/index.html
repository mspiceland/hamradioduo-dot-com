Pebble HF Programmer Quickstart

Welcome to your new Pebble HF Programmer! This quickstart will get you started with building and using your programmer to flash firmware onto your Pebble HF.

Thank you for your purchase and we hope it makes updating your Pebble HF a breeze.

This is the ATMEGA ISP PROGRAMMER by Barb WB2CBA, also known as the Barb Programmer. This programmer can update the firmware on the Pebble HF (and other AVR devices) using the simple web-based updater.

Build Instructions for Barb's Programmer

Solder the included header onto the bottom of the board on the spot labeled ISP TARGET.

Updating Firmware using the Programmer

Quickstart Instructions

Quick Start Instructions using the web-based installer.

WARNING: Do not separately power your Pebble HF device.
  1. Connect this adapter to your computer with a USB cable.
  2. Connect the adapter to the programming header on your Pebble HF.
  3. Open the web updater: https://mspiceland.github.io/pebblehf-web-firmware-updater/
  4. Choose the firmware.
  5. Click "Connect & Flash".
  6. Wait for it to finish.

For more details, see the detailed documentation.
