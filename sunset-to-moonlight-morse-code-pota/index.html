Our "Just OK" Mini Antenna Adventure

Our adventure proved that sometimes "just OK" is more than enough! With this compromised little antenna, we made 11 contacts across different states while getting to enjoy the evening. Could we have done better with a full sized antenna? Yes. Did we still get it done. Yes.

For this activation, we used our CFT1 5-Band CW Field Transceiver. It never disappoints with a wonderful sound and good performance. It works well paired with our USB-PD battery.
