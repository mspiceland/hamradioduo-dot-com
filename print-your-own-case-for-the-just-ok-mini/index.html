Print Your Own Case for The Just OK Mini

Mike and Becky December 14, 2025

If you have one of our Just OK Minis, the ridiculously small 20m antenna, and a 3D printer, you can print this little case we made for it.

This is more of a holder than a case, but it seems to keep it tidy for us in our small QRP radio bag. The 3D printed base snaps into one side and the whip snaps into the other side. The radio fits nicely on top.
