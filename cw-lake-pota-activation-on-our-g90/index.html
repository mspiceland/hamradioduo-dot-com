CW Lake POTA Activation on our G90

Mike and Becky March 14, 2025

Discover Lake Guntersville State Park

Lake Guntersville State Park is a wonderful favorite spot in Alabama. It's a great place to camp and play radio. We found a pavilion by the lake, providing a picture-perfect backdrop for a Parks on the Air (POTA) adventure. We had a great time and definitely recommend it as a place to visit.

Our Exciting Activation Day

On a brisk almost-still-winter day, we set up camp by the lake with our trusty Xiegu G90. We powered through the chill and had a great time! In no time, we had 61 contacts from hunters across the states. Our G90 and Becky's beloved Putikeeg Straight Key in hand and drew in both new and familiar calls. We even shared a fun Park-to-Park contact with friends from POTA Power. Thanks to Junie, Mochie, and Julie for sharing your clip!

If you want to catch the energy and fun of our windy activation day, check out the video below!

The Gear that Made It Happen

Here’s the awesome gear that helped make our activation a big success:

  • Xiegu G90
  • Xtenna EFHW Antenna
  • Becky's Putikeeg Straight Key
  • POTA20 Mast
  • Mast Holder / Ground Spike

This gear didn’t just help us communicate; it helped us connect with friends both near and far!

Join us in celebrating the joy of community and code at Lake Guntersville State Park. It's more than just a park activation—it’s about friendship and fun. Hope to catch you on the air soon. 73!
