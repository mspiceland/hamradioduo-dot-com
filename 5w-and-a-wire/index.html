Michael and Becky August 26, 2024
#Import 2024-11-22 21:56

We visited Washington near Seattle in the Pacific Northwest. We'd heard that the Rattlesnake Ledge had some of the most amazing views at it's overlooks. We went on a hike up 1400+ feet to check out the three steller overlooks.

Of course we brought our radio gear. We have a pack that is about 1 lb comprised of a QCX-mini, EFHW wire antenna, little battery, and tiny K6ARK paddle.
