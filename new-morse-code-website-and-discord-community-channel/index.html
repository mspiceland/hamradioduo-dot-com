✳️ New Morse Code Website and Discord Community Channel ✳️

Mike and Becky July 13, 2025

Join the new Morse Code Community!

Are you interested in learning morse code or know someone who is?! Michael and I have launched a brand new website that’s your go-to place for all things related to Morse code. We created this to be a one stop link for anyone interested in Morse Code.

https://learnmorsecode.org/

Our site is packed with resources to help you practice and hone your CW skills. We organized it so that it is easy to get started and easy to find the in depth videos when you are ready to dive in. Plus, it has tons of learning resources, clubs and communities to meet new people, and even games!

Join Our Lively Discord Community

We also created a Discord Community to meet other people also learning or into morse code. Join our Discord channel, "Learn Morse Code", at the bottom of the page, to chat with other Morse code learners just like you. Share your progress, ask questions, and make connections in a vibrant and supportive community.
