Sneak Preview: New Ham Radio Tools!

Mike and Becky December 1, 2024

First off, thank you for subscribing to our newsletter. To date, we only post a newsletter when we make a blog post to go along with our videos. We'd like to start sharing more and we're thankful that you subscribed to get updates.

We're excited to share that we created a new tools section on our website.

https://www.hamradioduo.com/tools/

We have a few tools there now:

  • POTA Map and trip report creator
  • CW Bot for virtual QSOs with an AI bot
  • Vail the CW practice tool with a decoder added

None of these tools are ready for prime time. I'm sure they are full of bugs. As a thank you for subscribing, we wanted to share them with you early in case you wanted to check them out and give us feedback.
