CW Clubs, Keys, and More from Hamfest 2024

Michael and Becky August 21, 2024
#Import 2024-11-22 21:56 Video

This was our very first Hamfest. We wanted to give anyone who couldn't attend a quick taste of some of the aspects for CW (morse code) opperators. We take a look at some neat keys along with some great interviews with CW clubs, LICW and SKCC. We also talk to Tony, the owner of N3ZN keys. Watch as Becky tries a bug for the very first time and see what we walk away with!
