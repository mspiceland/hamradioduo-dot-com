How is your fist?

Mike and Becky April 4, 2025

CW Morse Code Checker Tool

Of course we don't mean how is your literal fist, but how is your CW fist? Becky recently got a new Vibroplex Bug from the '60s. One of the first steps was to adjust everything and make sure that it works well. After watching some videos online, and one in particular from Adam AI4UH, we learned that there are CW fist checking apps for Windows that make fine business measuring the exact timing for adjusting your bug.

Unfortunately, we don't have access to Windows machines. Since we already have a couple of tools that use the vband adapter that lets you connect your key directly to your computer, we went down a rabbit hole and popped out the other side with a new tool!

Perfect Your Morse Code Using the CW Checker

We actually believe the best way to send good code is primarily listening to good computer generated code and then trying to send the same thing by sound. We even record ourselves and listen to ourselves. Still, after realizing what the fist checker does, we realized it would be really handy to be able to visualize how close we were to computer generated Morse code.

So, what does this CW Checker do? If you love Morse code like we do, you’ll appreciate this tool that works right in your browser. It checks your Morse code sending with a VBand or Vail adapter. As Becky practices with her new bug, we've realized how important it is to get your dit sending speed and dit-to-dah ratio perfect. This tool is your digital coach, helping you master those codes.

What You Need to Get Started

Ready to jump in? Here's a list of the tools you'll need from our video:

  • Vibroplex Presentation Model - 1965: A timeless classic.
  • JJ-38 Straight Key: Sometimes, simple is best. Since this video we also got a WW II vintage J-38, more details on that another day.
  • Vband Adapter: Available in the “store” at HamRadio Solutions.
  • Vail Adapter: It's open source! Check it out on GitHub.
  • Lightning to USB Adapter for iPhones: Get connected with ease!

If you’re curious about perfecting your Morse code, dive into our video and see the CW Checker in action!
