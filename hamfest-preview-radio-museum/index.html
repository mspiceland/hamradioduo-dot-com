Hamfest Preview: Radio Museum!

Mike and Becky July 29, 2025

Explore the Signals Museum in Huntsville

This week we had the honor to meet and interview Dr. Marc Bendickson who is the collector behind the new Signals Museum in Huntsville, AL. Just walking in the door you know it's going to be amazing. It's way more than just old radios; it's a treasure trove of stories and a tribute to radio history. The museum spans about 200 years of communication history and each stage has working equipment and hands on stations to learn even more about history.

The self guided tour enhanced with augmented reality characters are an extra bonus of technology built right into your experience. There are also tour guides around the museum to walk you through and explain even more.

Hamfest Discounts

If you're in town for the Huntsville Hamfest or just driving through, make sure to stop by the Signals Museum. It’s a must-see for fans of electronics and radio magic. As you explore, you’ll feel like you’re stepping back in time, with each display sharing a piece of radio’s remarkable past.

There are many hands on displays that let you battle with morse code, be a switchboard telephone operator, and see technology in action.

For details on tickets, special event Hamfest hours and discounts, visit their website at Signals Museum. Trust us, it’s an awesome side trip for your Hamfest weekend!
